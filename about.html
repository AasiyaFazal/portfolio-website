My Story

Hi, I am Aasiya, an aspiring UI/UX designer and I hope to be able to create memorable, fun, effortless experiences for people whilst making the most of my life, travelling the world.

My interest in UI/UX was fuelled by my first insight during an internship at Cancer Research UK. At the time, I had already committed to a job in the NHS starting straight after this internship, but I didnâ€™t want to close the door on a UI/UX career, therefore, I embarked on a part time course at CareerFoundry. Fast forward to today and I have a portfolio of projects I am proud of and the certainty that a career in the UI/UX field is what I want to pursue, having left my career in the NHS in May 2022.

I had the determination and resilience to see my UI / UX course through whilst working at one of the most pressurised times in the NHS during COVID and whilst pursuing a leadership postgraduate diploma in parallel. Therefore, I am confident that I can learn at a fast pace and thrive in this field.

With my previous professional experience spanning across data analytics, technology consulting, operational management, service design and project management, I have a versatile skillset to complement my design skills.
