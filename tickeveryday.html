    Overview

    This is an iOS app which aims to reframe people’s perspective on to do lists. This app is about making it easy, intuitive and fun to keep to do lists so that people have the time and clear headspace to think creatively and innovatively. This will allow them to generate more lightbulb ideas and make these a reality.

    Competitor Analysis

    Three existing to do list apps were analysed to understand the key features required within my app. This also gave insight into improvements which could me made to my app design to provide users with a better user experience than existing apps. The apps analysed were: Things 3, TickTick and Trello. A snapshot of the analysis is shown below.

    TickTick

    My app’s onboarding page was inspired by this one with improvements such as: a more compact layout for single sign on options, having a Facebook login and continue as a guest options.

    Things 3

    This app's search bar was incorporated into my design. The app categorises tasks in lists but doesn’t show any high level information about them. This was improved on in my design.

    Trello

    This app’s layout for editing a task inspired the design of the page in my app with small additions such as: a priority field, the ability to add tasks to a section of a list and a reminder field.
